BMW M5 COMPETITION

The BMW M5 is a high-performance version of the BMW 5 Series, developed by BMW M GmbH since 1984. The current generation (G90/G99), introduced in 2024, features a 4.4L twin-turbo V8 plug-in hybrid powertrain producing 717 hp (535 kW) and 1,000 Nm of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive (xDrive). It delivers a 0–100 km/h acceleration time of 3.5 seconds and a top speed of 305 km/h

BMW M4 COMPETITION

The BMW M4 is a high-performance version of the BMW 4 Series, developed by BMW M GmbH since 2014. The current generation (G82/G83), introduced in 2021, features a 3.0L twin-turbo inline-six engine producing 473 hp (353 kW) in the standard M4 and 503 hp (375 kW) in the M4 Competition, paired with a 6-speed manual transmission (standard M4) or an 8-speed automatic transmission (M4 Competition) and rear-wheel drive. It delivers a 0–100 km/h acceleration time of around 4.1 seconds for the standard M4 and around 3.8 seconds for the M4 Competition, with a top speed of 250 km/h (electronically limited).
